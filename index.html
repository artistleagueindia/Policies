Payment Policy & Confirmation

Payment must be completed through the official payment gateway integrated on the registration page (PhonePe or partner gateway).

No-refund policy

All registrations are final. Payments made are non-refundable under any circumstances except where the organizer cancels the event (see section 4). By registering you accept the no-refund policy.

Event Cancellation & Rescheduling

If the organizer cancels an event, attendees will be offered either a full credit for a future event or a partial refund at the organizer’s discretion. If the event is rescheduled, your registration will be moved automatically to the new date. The organizer will make reasonable efforts to inform registrants.

Transfer & Substitutions

Registrations are generally non-transferable. If you need to transfer a registration to another person, contact us at artistleagueindia@gmail.com at least 7 days prior to the event. Organizer approval is required.

Registration is automatically submitted only after payment confirmation. Manual entries are not accepted.

You will receive an automated confirmation email with an Order ID after successful payment — keep it for verification.

For disputes about payments contact: artistleagueindia@gmail.com with subject “Payment Dispute — {{ORDER_ID}}”.

Coupon Policy

  • Coupon list is private and validated on the server. Codes are single-use per email/phone.
  • Coupons apply only to the Full Camp package unless explicitly listed for other packages.
  • Coupons cannot be combined.
  • Organizers reserve the right to revoke coupon discounts if misuse is detected.

Privacy Policy (short)

We store basic contact information (name, email, phone), city, selected package, coupon usage, and payment record for event administration.

We use Google Sheets & Drive to store registrations and proof-of-payment where required.

We will not sell your personal data. We may send event-related emails and occasional announcements. To opt out of promotional messages reply to artistleagueindia@gmail.com.

By registering you agree to this data usage for event administration and communication.

Code of Conduct (brief)

  • Respect instructors and fellow participants. Harassment, discrimination, threats or offensive language will not be tolerated.
  • Respect venue property and equipment.
  • Follow any safety instructions provided by staff.
